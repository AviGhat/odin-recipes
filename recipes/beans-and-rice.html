Cajun Style Beans and Rice

Description

A nice yummy dish consisting of beans and rice!

Ingredients

  • White Rice or Brown Rice
  • Oil
  • vegetables
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Red Kidney Beans

Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain kidney beans
    2. After they have soaked overnight
  2. Sautee onion, bell pepper and celery
    3. In a large pot over medium heat for 10 minutes
  3. Add in seasonings
    4. add your parsley, hotsauce, salt and pepper and whatever else seasonings you like!
  4. Pour in kidney beans, bay leaves and veggie broth!
    5. bring to a boil then cover and lower the heat to a simmer and let everything cook
    for about an hour and 15 minutes then remove the lid and let it continue to simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Prepare your rice
    6. while the beans are cooking.
  6. Blend 1/4 of the cooked beans in a blender
    7. Blend until smooth.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
