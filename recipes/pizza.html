Pan-style Cheese Pizza

Description

A classic pizza made in a pan, which allows it to have thicker and fluffier slices!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Any toppings of your liking!
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cast iron pan!
  • Vegetable Oil

Instructions

  1. Kneed the dough
  2. Prepare the pan for pizza to cook. Oil it generously and put about 1 tablespoon semolina and rotate the pan to cover the base and edges.
  3. After 90 minutes, dough will be doubled in size
  4. Oil hands and start placing and flattening the dough to fit the pan
  5. Top each pizza with pizza sauce, and cheese and whatever other toppings!
  6. Cook pan pizza on for about 10-12 minutes
  7. ENJOY!
